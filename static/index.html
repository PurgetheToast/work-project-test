M.E.N.A.C.E Follows this series of steps:
1. The Human player finds the matchbox showing what the grid looks like at the current time, take a random coin out of the box that represents a place on the board and places its O or X
2. The Human player takes their move
3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until the game is finished
4. Then each move taken by menace during that game is either rewarded or punished by adding more coins if it has won or drawn using those moves, or punished by removing a coin if it has lost
